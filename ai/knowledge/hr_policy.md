# Uno HR Policy: Onboarding and Transfers

## 1. Onboarding Process
New employees must submit their documentation within the first 3 days of joining. The HR department will then create an Onboarding Order in the system.
- Eligibility check: Done automatically by the Product Service.
- System entry: Once approved, the employee is added to the Employee Center.

## 2. Transfer Policy
Employees can request a transfer after 6 months of continuous service in their current role.
- Approval: Requires consent from both current and receiving department heads.
- Quota: Transfers are subject to department headcount quotas managed in the Product Service.

## 3. Benefits and Welfare
Employees are entitled to health insurance and gym memberships based on their rank.
- Rank A/B: Full coverage.
- Rank C: 80% coverage.
